CM Punk Looks Forward to Facing Dominik Mysterio Now That Hes Reached Legal Age

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

CM Punk has been a huge draw after returning to WWE at Survivor Series last month and he has become progressively a bigger deal with every passing day, as fans continue to get more and more excited when they see him. He is set to square off against Dominik Mysterio this month and now Punk made it clear he can’t wait to destroy Mysterio.

The Second City Saint has appeared on Monday Night RAW, Friday Night SmackDown, and NXT Deadline after he returned to the company last month. It has become clear that Punk is simply a major draw for the company.

CM Punk is scheduled to enter the ring against Dominik Mysterio as part of WWE’s Live Holiday Tour on Tuesday, December 26th at Madison Square Garden. That’s not all as he is set to compete at Los Angeles’ KIA Forum against Dominik Mysterio on December 30th right after that.

CM Punk appeared at the Cage Fury Fighting Championships 128 event on Friday, making it clear WWE is allowing him to work there now. Punk hyped up his matches against Dominik Mysterio, stating that he can’t wait to destroy Mysterio now that he is of legal age.

“Watch me beat up ‘Dirty’ Dominik. I’ve been wanting to punch this kid in the face since he was eight years old. Now he’s legal and I’m gonna destroy him.” 

CM Punk has confirmed that he will be part of Monday Night RAW going forward. Punk also confirmed he will be competing in the Royal Rumble match next year, so we’ll have to see how Punk’s Road to WrestleMania will turn out next year.
