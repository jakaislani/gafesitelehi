Education

After completing his elementary school, Tanner joined a private school called Sacred Heart School in San Diego. He was only there for a year and a half before being expelled for filming a fellow classmate.

His father then put him in a charter school. He studied there for 2 years. By then, Tanner’s popularity had grown incredibly and he could not spare enough time for his studies. He took the GED Test which is the only recognizable credential equivalent to clearing high school. He cleared the test in 2015.
