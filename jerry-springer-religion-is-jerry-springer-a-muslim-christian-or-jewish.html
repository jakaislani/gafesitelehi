What was Jerry Springer Religion ?

Jerry Springer religion remains one of the interesting topics many have been asking about since his death announcement by his family. 

Jerry before passing away did not made it known himself which religion he belonged to. However we could trace his actual religion through the route of his parents. 

Jerry Springer parents were German Jewish refugees who escaped from Landsberg an der Warthe, Prussia, Poland. 

So we can assume that, he has a connection with the Jewish religion through his parents.
