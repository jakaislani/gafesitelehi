The actress started her career with her appearance in music videos like “There Goes My Life” and “I wasn’t Prepared”. After that, she has appeared in some popular series in supporting roles like “Jack and Bobby” in 2004 and “The O.C.” in 2005.

After that, she made her appearance in many movies like “Drop Dead Sexy”, “Price To Pay”, “You are Here”, and “Alpha Dog” in small supporting roles.

She also appeared in many TV series like “Criminal Minds”, “Side FX”, “North Country”, and “Remember the Daze”. After that, she bagged the life-changing role in her career in the stoner comedy “Pineapple Express” in 2008 for which she gained huge popularity in the industry.
