How Many Brothers And Sisters Do Janet Jackson Had? Know Her Family Ethnicity

Janet Jackson was born on 16 May 1966 to her family in Gary, Indiana as the youngest child among her siblings. She comes from an African American ethnicity. Her parents had nine other kids before her.

She has two sisters, La Toya Jackson, and Rebbie Jackson. On the other hand, she has seven brothers, Michael Jackson, Randy, Jermaine, Tito, Bandon, Marlon, and Jackie. Their family was from the lower-middle class. Along with her siblings, she came up together and formed a band named The Jacksons.
